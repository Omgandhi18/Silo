## Description  (max 4000)
Silo is a calm, on-device home for everything you're thinking about buying.

See something you want? Share the link to Silo from Safari or any shopping app, and it lands on your shelf — quietly filling in its image, title, and price on its own. When you're ready to buy, one tap takes you back to the store. No accounts. No servers. No notifications nagging you to spend.

WHY SILO
• Save in a second — share any product link and it's captured instantly.
• It does the work — Silo fetches the image and price, so your shelf looks beautiful with zero effort.
• Stay organized — file your finds into simple, color-coded collections.
• Buy on your terms — your saved things wait patiently. No carts, no pressure.
• Calm by design — a warm, paper-and-ink interface that's a pleasure to browse.
• Truly private — everything stays on your device. No tracking, no analytics, no ads. Ever.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Share a product link to Silo (or tap + to add one yourself).
2. Watch it fill in with a picture and price.
3. File it into a collection, or leave it in Unsorted.
4. Open & Buy whenever the moment is right.

Silo is for the way you actually shop — slowly, thoughtfully, when it suits you. It's the wishlist that respects your attention.

Private by default: Silo has no account and no cloud. Your saved items never leave your device.

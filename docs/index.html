Silo

Your calm product stash.

Save it now. Buy it when you're ready.

Silo is a calm, private locker for the things you want to buy. Share a link from any app and it lands here — quietly enriched with its image and price — ready for whenever you are. No accounts, no servers, no noise.

  • Capture anything. Share a product link from Safari or a shopping app and it's saved instantly.
  • It fills itself in. Title, image, and price arrive on their own.
  • File with a touch. Color-coded collections keep your finds in order.
  • Buy when ready. One tap back to the store, on your schedule.
  • Entirely yours. Everything stays on your device.
